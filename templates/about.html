About Entebbe Flying School LTD

Welcome to Entebbe Flying School LTD, where your aviation dreams take flight. We are dedicated to providing world-class training to aspiring pilots and aviation professionals in the heart of Uganda.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, safe, and professional aviation training through modern technology and expert instruction, preparing our students for successful careers in the global aviation industry.

Why Choose Us?

  • Expert Instructors: Learn from industry veterans with extensive flight experience.
  • Modern Facilities: We utilize the latest technology to ensure a comprehensive learning environment.
  • Safety First: Our training programs prioritize safety protocols that meet international aviation standards.

Our Vision

To be the leading aviation training center in East Africa, setting the benchmark for excellence in flight education and aviation management.
