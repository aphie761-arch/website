ADMISSIONS AT Entebbe Flying School LTD

We are currently accepting applications for the upcoming July intake. Join a premier training center dedicated to excellence in aviation.

HOW TO APPLY

  1. Submit Application: Complete our online inquiry form or visit our campus in Entebbe.
  2. Eligibility Review: Our team will review your academic qualifications and background.
  3. Interview: Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an assessment and personal interview.
  4. Enrollment: Once accepted, you will receive an offer letter to begin your training journey.

REQUIREMENTS

  • Completed application form.
  • Copies of academic transcripts and certificates.
  • Valid national identification or passport.
  • Medical fitness certificate from an authorized aviation medical examiner.

For more information, please contact our admissions office directly.
